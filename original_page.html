Solar: The Future Of Cheap Energy

According to the British Government’s own projections3, solar power is expected to become the 2nd cheapest form of electricity in the UK within the next few years.

Massive Benefits Of Going Solar in 2026

  1. 0% VAT – Homeowners can save hundreds on the cost of solar panels in 2026 thanks to the recent reduction in VAT.
  2. Lower up-front costs – New technology and more competition have dramatically reduced the cost of solar panels, making them an affordable option for millions of Brits.
  3. Slash your electric bills – Sunlight is free, so once you’ve paid for the panels you can start reaping the rewards. The savings can be up to hundreds of pounds per year!4
  4. Reduce your carbon footprint – Solar energy is one of the most environmentally friendly options out there.
  5. Increase EPC rating – Solar panels can significantly improve your property’s EPC rating.
